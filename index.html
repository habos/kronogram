About Kronogram

See all the wonderful features available!!

View your timeline

See a timeline of your internet history! We pull your Facebook, Twitter and Instagram data to make an aggregated timeline for you to share with your friends.

This day in history

See what people you follow posted on this day in history! View posts from last year to a decade ago and relive those precious memories.

Map your life

Create a map of all the places you have visited! Tag posts to different places to show how much of a traveller you really are!
